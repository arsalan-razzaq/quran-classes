About Tajweed Course

Do you feel the dire need to overcome your flaws in recitation and be fluent in Quranic Arabic? The correct and rightful recitation of the Quran holds great significance.

in the tajweed Quran course you will achieve this goals .

Reciting Quran out loud in a soulful voice would grant you many rewards. The Prophet (P.B.U.H) said”

‘Verily the one who recites the Qur’an beautifully, smoothly, and precisely, he will be in the company of the noble and obedient angels’
(Sahih Muslim)

To master the recitation of the Quran, one needs to excel in “Tajweed”. The literal meaning of “Tajweed” is to improve.

When taught with the Quran, it refers to enhancing our recitation of the Quran.

Tajweed constitutes the rules and regulations one needs to follow in recitation to make it beautiful and accurate.

Moreover, reciting Quran with proper Tajweed, in accordance with the proper rules and pronunciations, taking care of all the minor details and making sure nothing is missed out or pronounced differently.

Pronouncing Quranic words differently leads to a changed meaning, and it’s a sin to add or subtract from the true meaning of the Quran.

Tajweed is essential to follow and abide by to recite the Holy Quran rightfully and carefully.
